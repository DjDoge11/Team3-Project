The core flow of our app is helping a student calculate their grades, GPA, and credits. 
The user begins in the landing screen with a brief description of the website. They can then use the Nav bar to navigate to the feature they would like to use. 
For example, a user enters the website, optionally signs in, then navigates to the GPA sections, enters their grades and clicks calculate. They can then view their GPA. 
The user is done once they have calculated their grade, GPA, or credits.
